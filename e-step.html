E-Step Calibration

Prepare

Before starting the E-Step calibration, ensure you have the following:

  • Ruler or Caliper
  • Tape or Marker Pen

How to

Follow these steps to calibrate the E-Steps:

  1. Pull out the Bowden tube
  2. Measure 120mm of filament
  3. Extrude 100mm of filament
  4. Measure the remaining filament
  5. Enter the result and update

Formula

\( E_{\text{new}} = E_{\text{current}} \times \frac{L_{\text{measured}}}{L_{\text{expected}}} \)
