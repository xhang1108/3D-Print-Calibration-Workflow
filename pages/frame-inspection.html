Frame Inspection

Why is Frame Inspection Important?

A properly aligned and stable frame is crucial for achieving high-quality 3D prints. Frame inspection helps identify and correct issues that could affect print quality.

Inspection Steps

  1. Check frame stability
    • Ensure all bolts and screws are tight
    • Look for any wobbling or movement in the frame
  2. Verify frame squareness
    • Use a square tool to check all corners
    • Measure diagonals to ensure they are equal
  3. Inspect belts and pulleys
    • Check belt tension
    • Ensure pulleys are properly aligned
  4. Examine the print bed
    • Check for levelness
    • Ensure smooth movement along all axes

Tools Needed

  • Square tool
  • Measuring tape or ruler
  • Allen wrenches
  • Screwdriver set

Tips

  • Perform frame inspection regularly, especially after moving the printer
  • Address any issues immediately to prevent print quality problems
  • Consider using vibration dampers to improve stability
