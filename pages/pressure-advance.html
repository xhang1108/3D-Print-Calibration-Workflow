Pressure Advance

What is Pressure Advance?

Pressure Advance is a feature that compensates for pressure buildup in the extruder, reducing oozing and improving print quality, especially at corners and during rapid direction changes.

Calibration Process

  1. Enable Pressure Advance in your firmware
  2. Print a calibration pattern with varying Pressure Advance values
  3. Examine the pattern and identify the best value
  4. Set the optimal Pressure Advance value in your firmware
  5. Print a test model to verify improvements

Benefits of Pressure Advance

  • Sharper corners and edges
  • Reduced stringing and oozing
  • Improved dimensional accuracy
  • Ability to print at higher speeds
