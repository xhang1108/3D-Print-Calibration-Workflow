Temperature Tuning

Why is Temperature Tuning Important?

Proper temperature settings are crucial for achieving optimal print quality, strength, and surface finish. Different filaments require different temperatures for best results.

Tuning Process

  1. Start with the manufacturer's recommended temperature range
  2. Print a temperature tower or multiple test prints at different temperatures
  3. Evaluate each temperature level for:
    • Layer adhesion
    • Surface quality
    • Stringing
    • Bridging performance
  4. Choose the temperature that provides the best overall results
  5. Fine-tune if necessary

Tips

  • Different colors of the same filament may require slightly different temperatures
  • Consider both nozzle and bed temperatures
  • Re-tune when changing filament brands or types
  • Keep a log of optimal temperatures for different filaments
