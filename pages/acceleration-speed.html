Acceleration and Speed Tuning

Why Tune Acceleration and Speed?

Proper acceleration and speed settings can significantly improve print quality and reduce printing time. It's about finding the right balance between speed and quality.

Tuning Process

  1. Start with conservative settings (e.g., 500mm/sÂ² acceleration, 50mm/s speed)
  2. Print a test model (e.g., a 20mm calibration cube)
  3. Gradually increase acceleration and speed
  4. Observe print quality and listen for any unusual sounds
  5. Find the optimal settings where quality is maintained at higher speeds

Factors to Consider

  • Printer frame rigidity
  • Belt tension
  • Stepper motor capabilities
  • Filament type and quality
  • Desired print quality

Tips

  • Different models may require different settings
  • Consider using different profiles for speed and quality
  • Monitor your printer closely when testing high speeds
  • Remember that faster isn't always better - find the right balance
