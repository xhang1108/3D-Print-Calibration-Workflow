PID Autotune

What is PID Autotune?

PID Autotune is a process that helps your 3D printer maintain consistent temperatures for the hotend and bed. It optimizes the Proportional, Integral, and Derivative values for better temperature control.

Why is it Important?

Proper PID tuning ensures:

  • Stable temperatures during printing
  • Reduced temperature fluctuations
  • Better print quality
  • Increased energy efficiency

How to Perform PID Autotune

  1. Connect to your printer via USB or OctoPrint
  2. Ensure the hotend is at room temperature
  3. Send the PID autotune command (e.g., M303 E0 S200 C8 for hotend)
  4. Wait for the process to complete (may take 10-15 minutes)
  5. Save the new PID values to EEPROM

Tips

  • Perform PID autotune for both hotend and bed
  • Re-tune after changing nozzles or making significant changes to your printer
  • Ensure proper cooling during the tuning process
