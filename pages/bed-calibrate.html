Bed Calibration

Why is Bed Calibration Important?

A properly calibrated bed ensures good first layer adhesion and overall print quality. It's crucial for successful 3D printing.

Calibration Steps

  1. Heat the bed to your usual printing temperature
  2. Use the printer's leveling system (manual or automatic)
  3. Adjust the four corners of the bed
  4. Check the center of the bed
  5. Print a test pattern to verify calibration

Tips

  • Use a feeler gauge or paper for manual leveling
  • Perform bed calibration regularly
  • Consider using a glass bed for a flatter surface
  • Clean the bed surface before calibration
