Max Flow Calibration

What is Max Flow?

Max Flow is the maximum rate at which your printer can extrude filament while maintaining print quality. It's crucial for optimizing print speed and quality.

Calibration Process

  1. Design or download a max flow test model
  2. Start with conservative flow rates
  3. Print the test model, increasing flow rates gradually
  4. Observe print quality and look for signs of under-extrusion
  5. Identify the highest flow rate that maintains good quality
  6. Set your slicer's max volumetric speed accordingly

Factors Affecting Max Flow

  • Nozzle diameter
  • Hotend design
  • Filament type and quality
  • Extruder capabilities
  • Print temperature
