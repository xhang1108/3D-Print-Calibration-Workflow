Z-Offset Calibration

What is Z-Offset?

Z-Offset is the distance between the nozzle and the print bed when the Z-axis is at the home position. Proper Z-Offset ensures good first layer adhesion.

Calibration Process

  1. Home all axes
  2. Disable stepper motors
  3. Move the nozzle to the center of the bed
  4. Adjust Z-Offset until a piece of paper barely slides between the nozzle and bed
  5. Save the new Z-Offset value
  6. Print a test pattern to verify

Tips

  • Perform Z-Offset calibration after bed leveling
  • Re-calibrate after changing nozzles or print surfaces
  • Use live Z-adjust during the first layer of a print for fine-tuning
